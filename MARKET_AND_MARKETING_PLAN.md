# Digital Munshi ERP — Market Research & Marketing Plan (FY 2026–27)

## 1. The market (kitna bada hai)

India mein 6.3 crore+ MSME hain aur GST registered businesses 1.4 crore+. Billing/accounting software ka adoption abhi bhi ~10-15% hai — yaani **bahut bada untapped market**. 2026 mein pressure badh raha hai: faster GST compliance cycles, mandatory e-invoicing (turnover ghat raha hai threshold), e-way bills, multi-GSTIN.

- **Vyapar**: 1 crore+ (10 million) SMEs use karte hain — proof ki demand massive hai.
- Micro/retail/traders ka biggest base abhi **Vyapar, myBillBook, Marg**.
- Mid/established → **TallyPrime, Zoho Books, ClearTax**.
- Enterprise → SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Cloud, NetSuite, MS Dynamics, Odoo, Vasy ERP, Infor, Epicor.

## 2. Competitor map (kaun kya karta hai)

| Software | Target | Pricing | Strength | Weakness (humara mauka) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Vyapar** | Micro/retail | Lifetime-free basic + paid | Simple, offline, sasta | Basic; HR/payroll nahi; premium features paywalled; UI purana |
| **TallyPrime** | Established | ~₹18,000 one-time | Deep accounting, trusted | Seekhna mushkil, mobile/cloud weak, mehnga |
| **Zoho Books** | Service/SMB | Free <₹25L turnover | Cloud, GST API filing | Product/retail ke liye kam, ecosystem lock-in |
| **myBillBook/Marg** | Retail/distribution | Paid tiers | Distribution, barcode | Generic, limited customization |
| **SAP/Oracle/NetSuite/Dynamics** | Enterprise | ₹lakhs/year | Full ERP | SMB ke liye over-kill, mehnga |
| **Odoo/Vasy** | Mid-market | Modular | Customizable | Implementation heavy |

**Khaali jagah (positioning gap):** Vyapar (bahut basic) aur Tally/Zoho (complex/mehnga) ke **beech** — ek aisa SaaS jo *Vyapar jaisa simple* ho par *Tally jaisa powerful + Zoho jaisa cloud*, **aur HR/payroll, advanced multi-unit billing, godown, batch, WhatsApp-first** sab built-in. Yahi Digital Munshi ki jagah hai.

## 3. Digital Munshi ke unique selling points (USP)

1. **All business types ek hi app se** (settings se switch) — kirana, wholesale, restaurant, pharmacy, services, manufacturer, movers.
2. **WhatsApp-first** — invoice/estimate/reminder seedha customer ke WhatsApp pe.
3. **Built-in HR & Payroll** — Vyapar/myBillBook mein nahi (alag software lagta hai).
4. **Advanced billing** — multi-unit, primary↔secondary auto-calc, MRP-wise batch discount, multi-level discount (jo Vyapar premium mein deta hai).
5. **GSTIN verify + party documents + debtor/creditor** — onboarding fast.
6. **Desktop + Mobile + Cloud** — ek subscription, har device.
7. **Hindi + simple UI** — chhote dukaandar ke liye.
8. **Per-user SaaS pricing** — predictable, no big upfront (Tally ka ₹18k barrier nahi).

## 4. Pricing strategy (recommend)

- **Free trial 7 din** (already built).
- **Starter** ₹199–299/month (1 user) — billing + inventory + GST.
- **Pro** ₹499–699/month (3 users) — + HR/payroll, multi-godown, reports.
- **Business** ₹999–1499/month (unlimited users, multi-firm) — + e-invoice, API, priority support.
- **Yearly = 2 months free** (annual lock-in retention).
- Positioning: "Vyapar se powerful, Tally se sasta aur aasaan."

## 5. Target customers (kis ko bechna hai)

1. **Kirana / general stores** (sabse bada volume).
2. **Wholesalers / distributors** (multi-unit, godown — humari strength).
3. **Pharmacy** (batch/expiry — humari strength).
4. **Service businesses** — movers, repair, salons (HR/payroll + service billing).
5. **Restaurants** (KOT/POS).
6. **Small manufacturers** (raw material → finished, future).

## 6. Go-to-market (marketing plan)

**Phase 1 — Local + digital (0-6 months):**
- WhatsApp/YouTube demos in Hindi ("2 minute mein invoice").
- Local market visits + reference: ek shop ko free setup → woh 10 ko bole.
- Google/Meta ads on "GST billing software", "Tally alternative", "kirana billing app".
- SEO blog: "Vyapar vs Digital Munshi", "best GST software for [city]".

**Phase 2 — Channel + referral (6-12 months):**
- CA / accountant partner program (commission per client).
- Referral: refer karo → 1 month free dono ko.
- Vertical landing pages (kirana, pharmacy, movers).

**Phase 3 — Scale:**
- Reseller/franchise network (Tally ka model).
- App Store / Play Store presence.
- e-Invoice/e-Way GSP partnership.

**Marketing hooks (ads ke liye):**
- "Tally ₹18,000 — Digital Munshi ₹199/month se."
- "WhatsApp pe invoice bhejो, payment yaad dilao — automatically."
- "Dukaan + Staff + GST — ek hi app mein."

## 7. Product roadmap (feature parity + aage)

**Ab build ho raha hai:** Purchase & Expense, POS quick-bill, Live Stock report, Payment reminders (WhatsApp), Multi-firm, Thermal 58mm print, Command (Ctrl+K) search.

**Next 6 months:** e-Invoice/e-Way one-click (GSP), GSTR auto-filing API, mobile app (React Native), online store/payment links, loyalty points, recurring invoices.

**Differentiator bet:** **Voice billing in Hindi** ("Parle-G 2 packet 20 rupees") + **AI insights** ("is mahine Maggi dead stock, clearance karo") — yeh koi competitor strong nahi de raha, chhote dukaandar ke liye game-changer.
