How to use the GST calculator

Enter the amount and choose a GST rate. "Add GST" treats the amount as the base and adds GST on top. "Remove GST" treats the amount as GST-inclusive and extracts the base and tax. For same-state sales the tax splits into CGST + SGST; for inter-state, tick IGST.
