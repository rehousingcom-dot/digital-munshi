About Digital Munshi

Digital Munshi is an all-in-one billing, GST invoicing, inventory, credit (udhaar), accounting, POS and online-store software built for Indian small businesses — kirana stores, wholesalers, pharmacies, restaurants, garment shops and service providers. It is a product of Reload Digital India.

Why we built it

Most Indian shopkeepers juggle a paper register for udhaar, a separate app for billing, and Excel for accounts. We built Digital Munshi to put everything a dukaandar needs in one simple, Hinglish app that works on any phone — no computer required. From the first GST invoice to committee (BC) management, it is designed around how Indian shops actually run.

What makes us different

  • Truly all-in-one: billing, inventory, udhaar, double-entry accounting, GST reports, HR/payroll, POS, online store and committee/BC — in one subscription.
  • Made for India: Hinglish interface, UPI payments, WhatsApp sharing, GST-compliant invoicing and e-way bill support.
  • Works on mobile: installable app (PWA), no PC needed. Your data is backed up to the cloud daily.
  • Unique features: AI Munshi assistant, customer digital khata, Bharosa credit score, supplier network and online committee bidding.

Trust & security

Your business data is private to your account, encrypted in transit, and backed up automatically every day. We offer a 7-day free trial with no credit card, and you can cancel anytime. Pricing is transparent and listed openly on our pricing page.
