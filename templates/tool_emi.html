How EMI is calculated

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n − 1), where P is the principal, r is the monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12 / 100), and n is the number of months. This calculator shows your monthly EMI, total interest and total amount payable.
